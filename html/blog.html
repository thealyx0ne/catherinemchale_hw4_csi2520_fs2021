Kitchn

Kitchn - A blog founded in 2005 by Maxwell Ryan and Sara Kate Gillingham

"Kitchn" is a blog that shares food recipes, cooking lessons, product reviews, and kitchen design/renovation advice to over 17 million readers. On their site, you can find recipes for nearly any occassion - they have anything from fries and soup to margaritas and casseroles. If you don't know how to cook, or don't have much experience around the kitchen, they have great tutorials and tips to help out people with any skill level in the kitchen. You can choose the ingredients that you are interested in cooking with and find recipes for chicken, pork, salmon, eggs, rice, and even find substitute ingredients if you have dietary restrictions or preferences. The site also features things such as food history and news, holiday themed dishes, storage ideas for kitchens, and cuisisnes from countless cultures. There are also events that are occasionally held, which can include recipes and tutorials for upcoming holidays and events. If you love food, this is the site for you!
